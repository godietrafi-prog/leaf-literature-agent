Enzyme-assisted extraction (targeting hemicellulose via xylanase and cellulose via cellulase, both structural cell-wall components) produced the largest relative yield gain of any single intervention found in this pass: +163% to +209% versus untreated, with purity reaching 78.52% at the cellulase condition - both numbers clear this project's targets on their own if they hold up and are reproducible on carrot leaf tissue (which has its own distinct cell wall composition - would need direct testing, not assumed transfer). Mechanism (cell wall loosening/breakdown improving protein accessibility) is species-general and a strong candidate for the "digital twin" parameter space.
