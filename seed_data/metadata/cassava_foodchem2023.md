Notable mainly because "optimization" here still yields only 21.83% protein content and 18.31% extraction yield with 69% protein recovery yield - well below this project's purity target even after optimization, on a leaf system that (like carrot) has significant anti-nutritional/toxic baggage (cyanogenic glycosides here; carrot has less of an analogous issue but shares the "difficult matrix" character). The detoxification step design may be methodologically informative even though the numeric outcome isn't a hit.
