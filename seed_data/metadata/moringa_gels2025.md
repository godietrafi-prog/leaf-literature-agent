Full text obtained - all previously recorded numbers confirmed accurate. Two points worth adding beyond what was already known: (1) the product's color is now explicitly confirmed dark green and opaque, not off-white or light - so this paper's excellent yield (79.9%) and in-band purity (53.97%) come with an explicitly UNaddressed, and by the color numbers clearly unresolved, color problem; (2) the underlying CCD table (17 runs, Table 1) shows yield and purity are not simultaneously maximized by the same conditions - the model's individual-optimum stationary points for yield (90.48%) and purity (54.98%) occur at different parameter combinations, and the paper explicitly chose experimental Treatment 5 as a practical, reproducible balance point rather than either theoretical optimum - the same "balance point vs. individual optima" pattern seen in duckweed_pmc2023's own pH-precipitation choice.

This is now, together with cauliflower_fbt2026, one of two systems in this review with a genuinely well-verified (full-text, CCD-based, statistically analyzed) protocol landing inside the purity target band with yield well above target - but unlike cauliflower_fbt2026, this one has an explicit, unresolved color problem, and no chlorophyll or RuBisCO-specificity data at all.
