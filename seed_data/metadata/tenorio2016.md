Foundational review. Frames leaf protein recovery as three steps: disruption, separation, concentration. Key quantitative case study on sugar beet: thermal extraction (press -> 50C heat -> centrifuge) split total leaf protein almost equally across three streams - supernatant, green protein pellet, and fibrous pulp - meaning traditional soluble-protein-only methods leave the majority of total protein in the pulp/pellet fractions unextracted. Directly relevant to the yield target (>=30% recovery) since it quantifies the ceiling traditional methods hit.
